How We Can Help

All jobs under 10 minutes start at $5. Jobs that are typically this quick include:

  • Printer Jams
  • Frozen Computer
  • TV not showing picture
  • Documents won't open
  • Documents won't print
  • Internet not loading
  • Phone is frozen/Slow

Longer Services Include

  • Full Computer Reset
  • Fix phone pictures/contacts/apps
  • Setting up new printer
  • HardDrive/FlashDrive not recognized

We offer express service for quick and emergency needs, such as printer jams when you need to print an important paper, internet loss and when submitting assignments or other issues where you need our help right away. Our Doctors will contact you within 3-4 hours upon submittal.
